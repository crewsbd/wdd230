ABOUT US

Carlsbad is carls-good! That's what we though when we started our first store here in 1922. We are a small business who sell locally grown, organic food. That's what the cool kids eat. Although we started in Carlsbad, we have a strong presense along the southern California coast, from Los Angeles to San Diego.

Most of our food is sourced directly from farmers in the area. So you can get food from cool people who are like you, instead of weirdos somewhere else.
