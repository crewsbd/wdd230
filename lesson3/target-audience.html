HOME TARGET AUDIENCE DESIGN

Target Audience

Business owners and community members are the prime demographics that will benefit from the Fremont California Chamber of Commerce website.

Personas

Businesses - Any business owner in Fremont will want to be intimately familar with the Chamber of Commerce. It's the primary place for navigating inter-business initiatives and potentially establishing new ones. It's also a great place for new business owners to get a feel for the area.

Community Members - Any member of the Fremont community can also find value in the information provided on the Chamber of Commerces website. From learning about a local business to finding employment, there is a wealth of information to help while interacting with businesses.
